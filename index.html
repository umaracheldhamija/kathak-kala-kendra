The Guru

Guru Dharmendra JainKathak Guru & Tabla Maestro

✦

Guru Dharmendra Jain is a dedicated practitioner and teacher of Kathak — one of India's most luminous classical dance forms. Rooted in the ancient storytelling traditions of the temples of North India, Kathak weaves together rhythmic footwork, expressive gestures, and devotional narrative into a language of movement that transcends the ordinary.

Equally accomplished as a Tabla player, Guruji brings a rare musicianship to his teaching. His students benefit not only from precise technical instruction but from a deep understanding of taal, laya, and the inner pulse that animates both music and dance.

Based in the foothills of the Dhauladhar range in Dharamshala, Guruji has been shaping the cultural landscape of the region for over a decade — nurturing students of all ages and backgrounds with patience, rigour, and joy.

The Studio

Kathak Kala Kendra

A warm, dedicated space in the heart of Dharamshala where the ancient art of Kathak lives and breathes.

Nestled in the Natraj Guest House Building near Churan Khad, the Kendra is more than a dance school — it is a community of practitioners, a space of celebration, and a living repository of classical tradition. Students participate in performances, festivals, and cultural events throughout the year.
